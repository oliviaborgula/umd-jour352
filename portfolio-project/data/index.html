Multimedia

In spring 2023, I took JOUR370: Photojournalism, where I used a DSLR camera to capture action, feature and portrait photos. The course culminated in a 20-piece photo story, where I captured dancers at my university in motion during ballet class, rehersals and their final performance. These are some of my best photos.
