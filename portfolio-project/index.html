Welcome!


My name is Olivia Borgula. I’m an ambitious reporter studying journalism and information science at the University of Maryland. I’m passionate about writing, editing and data analysis. I’m pursuing a career as a reporter or data journalist and am looking to get more experience in the field.

I’m currently an assistant managing editor at The Diamondback, my university’s independent student newspaper. I help oversee a staff of more than 100 people and work 15 hours a week editing stories across all desks.

My writing has been featured in the Diamondback, Stories Beneath the Shell and DC Trending. Feel free to explore the rest of my page to see a comprehensive list of my writing, design and photography clips.
